ABOUT

Story

Al-Quran Academy is an E-Learning academy for teaching Qur'an, Tajweed ,Tafseer & Arabic online. All our teachers are graduated and authorized by Al-Azhar Organization to teach Qura'n,Tajweed,Tafseer and Arabic. We have both male and female teachers, for adults & children. Our vision is to spread the right way of reading Allah's Book (Al-Qur'an) .It is an honor to help you build a lifelong with Arabic and Quran.

Our Classroom

We provide a one on one class for teaching practical Quran Tajweed and Arabic Our courses provided in virtual classroom format allow students to interact actively with engaging and animated slides. In addition to that, our teachers guide students through each learning point and ensure that learning objectives are mastered. We train our teachers to deal with Muslims of different cultures and take a practical approach towards education.
